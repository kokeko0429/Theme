Participatory Conference 2018 student projects

Designing alternative disability employment system through usage of digital fabrication machines in Japanese welfare facilities

日本の障がい者支援施設における、デジタル工作機械の利活用による新たな障がい者就労支援のデザイン 参加型デザインを通じた商品開発がもたらす工賃の向上とやりがいの創出

About this project

This Research Through Design project aims to raise waged and provides employment support for people with disabilities,and help people find fulfilment and a feeling of independence in their everyday life.
